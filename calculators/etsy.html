Example Calculation

If you sell an item for $40, with a product cost of $15 and shipping of $5:

  • Listing Fee: $0.20
  • Transaction Fee (6.5%): $40 × 6.5% = $2.60
  • Payment Processing (3% + $0.25): $40 × 3% + $0.25 = $1.45
  • Total Costs: $15 + $5 + $0.20 + $2.60 + $1.45 = $24.25
  • Net Profit: $40 − $24.25 = $15.75
  • Profit Margin: ($15.75 / $40) × 100 = 39.38%
