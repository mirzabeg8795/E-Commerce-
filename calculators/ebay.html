Example Calculation

If you sell an item for $60, with a product cost of $25 and shipping of $6:

  • Final Value Fee (~13.25%): $60 × 13.25% = $7.95
  • Total Costs: $25 + $6 + $7.95 = $38.95
  • Net Profit: $60 − $38.95 = $21.05
  • Profit Margin: ($21.05 / $60) × 100 = 35.08%
