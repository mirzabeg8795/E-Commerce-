Example Calculation

If you sell an item for $50, with a product cost of $20, FBA fee of $8, and a 15% referral fee:

  • Referral Fee: $50 × 15% = $7.50
  • Total Costs: $20 + $8 + $7.50 = $35.50
  • Net Profit: $50 − $35.50 = $14.50
  • Profit Margin: ($14.50 / $50) × 100 = 29.00%
